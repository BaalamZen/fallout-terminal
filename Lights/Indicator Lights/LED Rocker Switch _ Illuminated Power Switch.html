Description

We pity the fool . . . who buys sub-par rocker switches! 

Yeah, that fool was US. We snagged cheap-o rockers off Amazon to repair some audio equipment in the home studio (A Furman power conditioner to be precise), only to be reminded of the lesson we try to teach y'all which is: you can't trust stuff from Amazon! Those switches only lasted three months which makes sense because no one is checking to make sure they aren't factory rejects, cheap copies or sold by someone that has no idea what they are.

The good news for you is that we were obligated to start searching for some quality on/off rocker switches, which has led us here, to this very product page, and these absolutely worth-your-while LED rocker switches. 

Go ahead, compare these illuminated power switches to ones you'll find elsewhere on the web. Ours are rated for 30 amps (compared to 15A for most of the ones on Amazon) and 10,000 electrical cycles -- that means you could turn this switch on and off once a day for the next 27 years. We think that's a pretty solid $2.55 investment.

These 4-pin, 2-postion rocker switches provide a satisfying "click" in the On position. Choose your LED color, green or red.

The Specs:

Rocker switch

DPST: 4-pin, 2 position (on-off)

Illuminated with green or red LED

30A (AC) 250 V

Panel mount, snap-in 

Terminal type: Quick Connect - 0.250" (6.3mm) - can also be soldered

Panel cut-out dimensions: 30x22mm

TUV/CE/CB/CQC certified
